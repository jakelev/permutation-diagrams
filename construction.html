TCCW Construction

From TCCW diagrams w (size n) and v (size m) and an n-subset X of {2,…,n+m+1}, build the TCCW diagram u = w' 1 v' of size n+m+1.

Reversed if needed so w1 ≤ wn.
Reversed if needed so v1 ≥ vm.
X ⊆ {2,…,n+m+1}, |X| = n. Its complement is Y.
